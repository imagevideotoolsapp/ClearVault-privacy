4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Data Storage

All your media files and app data are stored locally on your device:

  • Compressed videos remain in your Photos library
  • App preferences are stored in UserDefaults
  • Thumbnail caches are stored in the app's document directory
  • No media files are uploaded to external servers

4.2 Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption: Data at rest is protected by iOS's built-in encryption
  • Sandboxing: The app runs in an isolated sandbox, protecting your data from other apps
  • Secure APIs: We use Apple's secure frameworks (Photos, AVFoundation, etc.)
  • No Cloud Storage: Your media never leaves your device

4.3 Data Retention

  • Media Files: Stored indefinitely on your device until you delete them
  • App Preferences: Stored until you delete the app
  • Crash Reports: Retained by Firebase for 90 days
  • Analytics Data: Aggregated and anonymized, retained for analysis purposes

4.4 When You Delete the App

When you uninstall ClearVault:

  • All app data and preferences are automatically deleted
  • Thumbnail caches are removed
  • Your original photos and videos remain untouched in your Photos library
  • Compressed videos you created remain in your Photos library (they are now part of your library)
